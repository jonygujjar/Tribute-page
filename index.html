Abdul Sattar Edhi

Abdul Sattar Edhi was a Pakistani philanthropist, ascetic, and humanitarian who founded the Edhi Foundation.

Here is Some Awards of Abdul Sattar Edhi

  • Ramon Magsaysay Award for Public Service
  • Lenin Peace Prize
  • Paul Harris Fellow from Rotary International
  • Hamdan Award for volunteers in Humanitarian Medical Services
  • International Balzan Prize (2000) for Humanity, Peace and Brotherhood
  • Peace and Harmony Award (2001)
